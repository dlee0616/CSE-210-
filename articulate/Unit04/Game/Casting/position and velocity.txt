position: (0, 0)

frame1: (0,0)
frame2: (1, -1)
frame3: (2, -2)

how it works:
velocity x and y are ADDED to the curret position x and y values to get the new position.

i.e.
position: (0, 0)
velocity: (3, 3)

frame1: (0,0)
    add velocity x and y to position x and y
frame2: (0+3, 0+3)
